What is NCET?

NCET is the national-level entrance exam for admission into ITEP (INTEGRATED TEACHERS EDUCATION PROGRAME) commonly knows as 4-year integrated BSc BEd programs at Regional Institutes of Education (RIEs) and Central Universities. Conducted by NTA, it replaced individual RIE entrance tests. For science stream students (PCB), (PCM), (COMMERCE) & (HUMANITIES), the relevant program is BSc BEd CBZ (Chemistry, Botany, Zoology), BSc BEd PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics), BA BEd & Bcom BEd.

Why RIE over other options?
✅ Dual degree (BSc + BEd) in 4 years vs 5 years separately
✅ NCERT-run — high credibility for KVS/NVS recruitment
✅ CBZ gives Botany + Zoology at graduation → qualifies for PGT Biology
✅ Low fees + hostel = minimal financial burden on family
Counselling Process
STEP 1
NCET Result — NTA releases scorecard. Download and save.
STEP 2
NCERT Counselling Portal Opens — Register on the official NCERT counselling website.
STEP 3
Choice Filling — Select RIE + program preferences in order of priority. You can fill multiple choices — allotment follows your rank and preference order.
STEP 4
Seat Allotment — Based on rank, category, and choice order. Multiple rounds possible.
STEP 5
Document Verification — Visit allotted RIE with original documents.
STEP 6
Fee Payment & Admission — Pay fees, confirm seat. Done! 🎉

How to Decide Your RIE Preference Order
📍 Proximity: Choose RIEs closer to your home state to reduce travel costs and homesickness.
🏫 All RIEs are equal in degree value — the BSc BEd degree from any RIE has the same NCERT-backed credibility for KVS/NVS recruitment.
📋 Fill all 5 RIEs in your preference order — don't leave choices blank. More choices = better chances of getting a seat.

Career Roadmap after NCET → RIE
YEAR 1–4 (BSc BEd)
BSc BEd CBZ / PCM — RIE or Central University
4-year integrated program. Subject specialization + B.Ed simultaneously. Central govt funded, very low fees, hostel available.
YEAR 5–6 (MSc)
MSc in relevant subject
Options: MSc Botany / Zoology / Biotechnology / Life Sciences from any central university. Required for PGT-level teaching jobs.
YEAR 7+ (Job)
KVS / NVS PGT Biology (or relevant subject)
Central govt school teacher. Stable salary, pension, Level 8 pay (~₹47k starting). High vacancy window 2025–2035 due to retirements.
ALTERNATIVE
State TGT/PGT · DSSSB · University Lecturer (NET/SET)
BSc BEd + MSc opens multiple teaching pathways at state and central level.
Why MSc Biotech qualifies for PGT Biology
KVS/NVS PGT Biology requires: MSc in Botany / Zoology / Biology / Life Sciences / Biotechnology (with Botany + Zoology at graduation).
BSc CBZ (Chemistry, Botany, Zoology) ✅ satisfies the "Botany + Zoology at graduation" condition. So MSc Biotech on top of CBZ = fully eligible.
